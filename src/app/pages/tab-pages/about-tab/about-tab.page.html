About Us

Rain or Shine (ROS) Elastomeric Waterproofing Paint is the flagship brand of Asian Coatings Philippines Incorporated. It is one of the leading paint manufacturers in the Philippines. The company started manufacturing high quality house paints in 1992 and with the foresight of its visionary leader, the company ventured into making superior elastomeric waterproofing paint in 2004 under the brand name Rain or Shine (ROS).
