Painting Tips

Points to Remember
  1. Determine the number of square meters to be painted.
  2. Divide the total number of square meters by the coverage per 4 liters of paint(the result is the number of 4 liters required).

Variables that affect spreading rates.
  1. Type and condition of surface.
  2. Tools used for application.
  3. Thinning.
  4. Individual application technique.

How to Get the Area of Different Shapes

1. RECTANGLE

Multiply the base measurement by height in meters

base x height = Area in square meters

2. TRIANGLE

Multiply the base measurement by height in meters and divide by 2

(base x height) / 2 = Area in square meters

3. CYLINDRICAL

Area of Cylinder + Area End Circles (1) Area of Cylinder

Multiply the circumference by the height in meters

(2) Area of Circle

Multiply the diameter by itself the multiply by 0.7854

Circumference = diameter x 3.1416

4. Downspouts and Gutters - calculate area as in cylinder
